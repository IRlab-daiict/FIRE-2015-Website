POSTER Session


We invite short papers (2 pages) describing work on any topic related to IR. Submitted posters will be blind-reviewed according to originality and technical merit. Selected papers will be published in the Working Notes and can be presented as posters during the Workshop. This will provide the authors with an opportunity to obtain direct feedback about their work from a wide audience during the poster session. Authors of selected short papers will be invited to submit an extended version for publication in the peer-reviewed post-proceedings to be published as an LNCS volume.


SUBMISSION


Extended abstracts of posters must be in English and should not exceed 2 pages in the ACM 2-column format (including references and figures). Suitable LaTeX and Word templates are available from the ACM Website: http://www.acm.org/sigs/publications/proceedings-templates (for LaTeX, use Option 2). The only accepted format for submissions is PDF. Poster papers must be submitted electronically using EasyChair at https://www.easychair.org/conferences/?conf=fire2012poster.


Author identity should be concealed (please omit author names and affiliations from the initial submission). Submissions that are over the 2-page limit or are not anonymous will be rejected without review. Note that summaries of full papers that have already been published elsewhere (including in another language) are not acceptable as poster submissions for FIRE.


At least one author must register and attend the conference. Please note that it is the responsibility of the authors to ensure that the PDF submission has been uploaded successfully (we suggest that you try downloading your paper again yourself, to check).
